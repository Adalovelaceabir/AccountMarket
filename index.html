Frequently Asked Questions

Is buying/selling social media accounts legal?

While the act of buying and selling accounts isn't illegal, it may violate the terms of service of some platforms. We recommend checking the specific platform's policies before engaging in transactions.

How does the escrow system work?

Our escrow system holds the buyer's payment securely until the account transfer is successfully completed. Only then is the payment released to the seller, ensuring protection for both parties.

What fees do you charge?

Listing accounts is free. We charge a 10% commission only after a successful sale. There are no hidden fees or upfront costs.

How do I determine the value of my account?

Value depends on factors like follower count, engagement rate, niche, monetization status, and account age. Our valuation tool can help estimate your account's worth based on recent sales data.
